Joe Marino

I am a Ph.D. student in the Computation and Neural Systems program at the California Institute of Technology (Caltech). I am co-advised by Professors Pietro Perona and Yisong Yue.

My focus is on combining deep learning and probabilistic graphical models while taking inspiration from neuroscience. The ultimate goal is create machines with tools that enable them to learn (almost) autonomously, i.e. with minimal support from humans.
